Sumukh Vasisht Shankar

I’m Sumukh Vasisht Shankar, Lead Data Scientist specializing in cardiovascular research, AI deployment, and natural language processing. I’ve led projects at Yale’s Cardiovascular Data Science Lab with Dr. Rohan Khera and Northeastern’s Geometric Learning Lab with Dr. Robin Walters.

My work spans deep learning, generative models, retrieval-augmented systems, Edge AI, and full-stack ML deployment with DevOps.
